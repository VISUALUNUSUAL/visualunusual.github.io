Museum of Eastern Mari Culture

Mari Cultural Center, Ufa, Russia
company: Media Minds & Media RB Engineering Group
Role: Art Director / Lead Designer

A small regional museum wanted to present the heritage of the Eastern Mari people in a way that would resonate with modern audiences while preserving cultural authenticity.

concept
produciton

We combined artifacts with a series of immersive media experiences. Visitors could explore cultural traditions through an interactive table with a dynamic calendar of Mari holidays, projection-based storytelling, touchscreens with accessible narratives, and ambient audio featuring Mari language and nature recordings. The visual identity was based on authentic patterns and traditional color palettes.

Despite its modest scale, the museum became an immersive cultural environment that preserves intangible heritage — language, music, and oral history — and makes it engaging for a broader audience.
