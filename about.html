I’m a designer and creative director working at the intersection of storytelling, technology, and space. I build interactive installations and immersive media for museums, exhibitions, and public environments — turning ideas into experiences people can walk through, touch, and remember.

Over the past 15 years, I’ve moved from visual communication to full-scale interactive projects: from projection-mapped walls to VR journeys and sensor-driven exhibits. I enjoy shaping a narrative that unfolds across space — whether it's in a museum hall or a responsive digital surface.

When I’m not working on client projects, I create generative visuals using code — currently exploring WebGL and AI-based tools as new ways to sketch with light and movement.

I’m always interested in new collaborations that connect design, emotion, and emerging technology.
