"Oil" — Multimedia Exhibition at VDNH

Client: VDNH | Lukoil
Company: Ascreen
Role: Lead Visual Designer (UI identity)

The pavilion needed to explain the complex oil production cycle to a diverse audience — from school groups to industry professionals — without overwhelming them with technical information.

We defined the visual language and interface system for all multimedia experiences in the pavilion. Working closely with curators, engineers, and developers, we ensured consistency across UI, on-screen storytelling, and spatial graphics.

The exhibition was structured into four thematic zones — History, Science, Technology, People & Oil — each incorporating interactive installations, touch-based applications, AR/VR experiences, holographic media, games, and educational films. 

The pavilion became one of the most engaging educational spaces at VDNH, offering visitors a hands-on understanding of the oil industry through intuitive multimedia and a unified visual identity.
